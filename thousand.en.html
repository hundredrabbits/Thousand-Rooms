Thousand Rooms

Thousand Rooms

Cat, Owl, Bat & Fox

Illustrated by Rekka Bellum
Written by Devine Lu Linvega

First Edition

You wake up in a room, surrounded by unbreakable walls. On a desk, lies a pencil and a sheet of blank paper.
Inside the desk, notes bearing your handwriting, you have no recollection.

Lesson 1

Cat awakens.

Owl finds a piece of paper.

Bat finds a pencil.

Fox opens the desk.

Lesson 2

Cat claws at the walls.

Owl leaves a message in the desk.

Bat finds a second pencil.

Fox dismantles the desk.

Lesson 3

Cat destroys the pencil and the paper.

Owl finds notes in the desk.

Bat finds the desk full with pencils.

Fox builds the desk inside-out.

Lesson 4

Cat enters the desk.

Owl tries to understand.

Bat sees no escape.

Fox has a visitor.

Lesson 5

Cat comes out of the desk.

Owl understands the room.

Bat awakens.

Fox has a second visitor.

Lesson 6

Cat destroys the desk.

Owl finds a scribble.

Bat finds a pencil.

Fox has too many visitors.

Last Lesson

What would you do?
